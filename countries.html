2022 Olympic Medals:

The Beijing 2022 medals are namend "Tong Xin" which means "together as one". The medals are composed of five rings and a centre. This design was inspired from the Chinese ancient jade concentric circle pendants, and the five rings represent the Olympic spirit.

Medal Design:

The Beijing 2022 medals have the five Olympic rings engraved on the front center of the medal. The words "XXIV Olympic Winter Games Beijing 2022" are engraved around it. Outside this ring, there is ice, snow, and cloud patters on the remaining rings of the metal.
